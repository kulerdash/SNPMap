rs333

SNP of the Month

The chemokine receptor gene CCR5 plays an important role in many immune-related processes. Delta 32 rs333, designating the CCR5-delta32 deletion of 32 nucleotides from within the gene, is perhaps the most famous allele of CCR5. Individuals carrying one copy of the delta 32 allele are somewhat resistant to infection by HIV, the virus that causes AIDS.
